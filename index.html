Kimchi

This recipe is curated from "Kimchi Recipe (Napa Cabbage Kimchi)" written by Sue, and found on My Korean Kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 4kg napa cabbage (Chinese cabbage)
  • 16 cups (3840 ml) water
  • 1.5 cups Korean coarse sea salt
  • 1/2 cup cooking salt
  • 2 Tbsp glutinous rice flour
  • 1.5 cups gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • 540g daikon radish, julienned
  • 1 Tbsp fine sea salt
  • 3.5 Tbsp Korean fish sauce
  • 2 Tbsp salted fermented shrimp
  • 90g Korean chives, cut 2-inch
  • 140g carrots, julienned
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp raw sugar
  • 75g onion, blended

Directions:

  1. Cut the napa cabbage into quarters and rinse it in running water.
  2. Dissolve the salt in water and soak the cabbage.
  3. Rub salt into the thick leaves, reserve the saltwater, and pickle for 6 hours.
  4. Rinse cabbages and drain for 1 hour.
  5. Make glutinous rice paste and mix with chili flakes.
  6. Combine with radish, fish sauce, shrimp, chives, garlic, and onion mixture.
  7. Spread seasoning over cabbage leaves and store in container.
  8. Ferment 24 hours at room temperature, then refrigerate.
